How to Use

- Microwave : Place momos on a microwave-safe dish. Place a cup of water along with the momos. Heat on high flame for 2 minutes for up to 5 pieces
- Steaming : Fill half the steamer pot with water and bring to a boil. Steam for 3 minutes for up to 5 pieces
- Fried Momo : Deep fry the delicious momos in hot oil till they turn golden brown and crisp. Serve with the Wow! Momo sauce
- Kothey Momo : Steam/Microwave the delicious momos and then put 3 tbsp. of vegetable oil in a non stick pan. Add the momos and put the flame on medium. Shallow fry them for a few minutes but do not turn them around. When golden brown, lift the delicious kotheys out and serve with the Wow! Momo sauce
