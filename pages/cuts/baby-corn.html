DESCRIPTION

Overview

Baby corns are small in size and handpicked before they mature.

Benefits

In addition to its delicious sweet taste, baby corns are high in dietary fiber, low in fat and a great source of essential nutrients. They are also high in potassium and folic acid.

How to Use

Baby corns have a wide variety of uses. Toss them on your pastas, vegetable soups, stir fries, Manchurian and stews. They can be eaten raw and the entire tiny ear of the corn is edible.
