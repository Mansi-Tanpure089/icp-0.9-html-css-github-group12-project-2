DESCRIPTION

Overview

- The Pigeon Digital Healthifry Air Fryer is a versatile kitchen appliance designed for healthy cooking, utilizing hot air to fry food with little to no oil.

Key Features

- Digital Controls: Features a digital interface for easy control of temperature and time.
- 4.2-Litre Capacity: Suitable for cooking a variety of dishes for medium-sized families.
- Rapid Air Technology: Ensures even cooking with minimal oil for healthier meals.
- Multifunctional: Ideal for frying, baking, grilling, and roasting various foods.

How to Use

- Preheat: Preheat the air fryer to the desired temperature before adding food.
- Cooking: Place the food in the basket, set the temperature and time, and start cooking.
- Cleaning: Allow the air fryer to cool before cleaning, then wash the basket and tray with warm, soapy water.

Benefits

- Healthier Cooking: Reduces the need for oil, making fried foods healthier.
- Quick and Easy: Cooks food faster than conventional methods, saving time.
- Energy Efficient: Consumes less energy compared to traditional ovens.

Storage Instructions

- Store in a dry, cool place when not in use.
- Ensure the appliance is clean and dry before storage.
