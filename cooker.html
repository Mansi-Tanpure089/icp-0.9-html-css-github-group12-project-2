DESCRIPTION

Overview

- The Bajaj Stainless Steel Outer Lid Pressure Cooker is a durable and efficient kitchen tool, ideal for quick and safe cooking.

Key Features

- Stainless Steel Body: Ensures durability and long-lasting performance.
- Outer Lid Design: Provides a secure seal for fast and efficient cooking.
- 5-Litre Capacity: Perfect for cooking meals for a family.
- Safety Features: Equipped with a pressure indicator and safety valve for safe cooking.

How to Use

- Add Ingredients: Place ingredients and required amount of water in the cooker.
- Seal the Lid: Secure the outer lid and place the cooker on the stove.
- Cooking: Wait for the pressure to build up, then reduce heat and cook for the required time.
- Release Pressure: Turn off the heat and allow the pressure to release naturally before opening the lid.

Benefits

- Time-Saving: Cooks food faster than conventional methods.
- Nutrient Retention: Helps retain more nutrients compared to boiling.
- Versatile: Suitable for cooking a variety of dishes, including rice, lentils, and stews.

Storage Instructions

- Clean thoroughly after each use and store in a dry place.
- Keep the lid and rubber gasket separate when not in use to maintain the seal's quality.
